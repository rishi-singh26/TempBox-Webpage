Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 6th September 2024

Tempbox does not collect any user data, so there is little to address in that regard. However, there are still a few points that should be clarified.

By using the TempBox application, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data collected by the app

The TempBox application does not collect any user data. TempBox relies on the mail.tm API to deliver its functionality. While this API maintains server logs, these logs are not classified as Personal Identifiable Information (PII). Users are encouraged to review the mail.tm privacy policy, available at https://mail.tm/en/privacy, for further details.

Third-Party Services

The TempBox application utilizes the mail.tm service to provide email functionality. It is recommended that you review the mail.tm privacy policy at https://mail.tm/en/privacy to gain a better understanding of their data handling practices.

Data Usage

As TempBox does not collect or store any personal data, no user information is utilized for analytics, marketing, or any other purposes.

Data Security

TempBox does not collect or process any user information. The email addresses and messages received by users are stored on servers managed by mail.tm. It is recommended to review their privacy policy, available at https://mail.tm/en/privacy, for further details.

Deletion of your data in the app

To delete your data, individual messages and email addresses must be manually removed. If you uninstall or delete the application without first deleting these messages and email addresses, they will remain stored on mail.tm servers.

Handling of your data on the website

This website (tempbox.rishisingh.in) does not collect any data from its visitors. It is hosted on GitHub Pages, and their policies apply. For more information, please review GitHub Inc.'s privacy policy available at github.com.
