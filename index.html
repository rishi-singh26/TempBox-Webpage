TempBox

A simple app to create Anonymous emails.
Quickly generate disposable addresses and keep your inbox private and spam free.

Create Addresses Instantly

Admittedly there are a lot of websites out there that ask for and account before you can test their service or just to access their content. Some of which start sending spam to your inbox. With this app you can quickly create a disposable email address so you can first try the service before signing up with your private email.

Login to Exisiting Addresses

If you have used the mail.tm web client or any other mail.tm client before and have a address, you can easily login and access that address.

Archive the address

Ones you are done with the address you can archive them, this will not delete the address but you will not receive emails anymore. If at a later point you want to start receiving emails again you can unarchive that address.

Export your addresses

You can export your addresses in bulk, this feature can be used to sync accounts between two platforms. Tempbox does not sync your data between different platforms.
